Let's dive into K-Pop online community and talk about K-Pop, through an analysis of YouTube comments collected from music videos of six multinational K-Pop groups.

Visualization: Immigrants and Korean Society

The immigrant issue has become part of the central concern in Korean society in recent years. This visualization project uses data from the Korean General Social Survey conducted by the Survey Research Center at Sungkyunkwan University in 2003 and 2013. I used Python open source library Dash and Plotly for the visualization, and the dashboard was designed to present the time trend shift of how different social groups in Korea see immigrant issues differently.
